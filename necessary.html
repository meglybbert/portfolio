POSTERS FOR KIDS WHO CAN'T WRITE GOOD

These pieces were the final products and comps for a "startup" assignment, which involved coming up with the idea for a possible startup, creating packaging for a good sold by the startup, and creating a print ad for the startup. The company, "Mrs. Lybbert's Posters for Kids Who Can't Write Good" would design manufacture posters that could be placed in high school and university classrooms and used as teaching aids, given that the majority of educational posters currently available are either poorly designed or are designed for a much younger audience.
