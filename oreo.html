OREO AD SERIES

This ad series acts as a wordless representation of the fun and irresistible element of the brand. The first three advertisements function as print ads, while the fourth is an environmental ad. Oreo stickers would be placed on black poker chips in casinos, acting as a tangible piece of advertising that would be handled by many viewers.
